Tal Nesher's Favourite Cocktails!

Here are my top 3 cocktails:

Breath Of Wine

A light and fresh cocktail based on dry white wine with some Branca Menta, gin and mint foam.

Zombi

A very strong classical Tiki cocktail with rums, grapfruit, cinnamon and more...

Negroni

Simply a classic, and for a reason. Campari, gin and sweet vermoth.
